About Us

We have been offering Taekwon-Do training at York University since 2008. The Taekwon-Do Club at York University is governed and run by York students. Our instructors are volunteers who came to us from Master Caputo's Beaches ITF school, in Toronto. It is thanks to Master Caputo's continuous support and guidance that those instructors are able to offer free training to our community.

Our head instructors are:

  • Mr. Francesco Spinelli - fifth degree black belt and International Instructor

  • Mr. Wojtek Zurek - third degree black belt Instructor and Coach

Both of them have extensive teaching, training and competitive experience. Each of them has trained Taekwon-Do for over 30 years and represented Canada in many international level competitions, including world championships, both as competitors and coaches.

We practice ITF style Taekwon-Do, but we welcome all students regardless of their current or past martial art experience and affiliation. At the least, we offer good, hard workouts and a fun training atmosphere that may greatly enhance and complement your academic experience at York University.

We invite you to join us in training.
